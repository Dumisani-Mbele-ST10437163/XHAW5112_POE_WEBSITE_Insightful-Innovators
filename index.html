Our Vision

Empowering the Nation" is a training initiative dedicated to providing skills development for domestic workers and gardeners. Founded by Precious Radebe in 2018, the business offers a variety of practical courses to help individuals upskill and become more marketable in their roles. By enhancing their abilities, these workers can increase their earning potential and even start their own businesses. The training covers a range of areas, from first aid, sewing, and landscaping to life skills, cooking, and child minding. The goal is to empower individuals with essential skills to improve their employment opportunities and quality of life
